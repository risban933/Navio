Welcome to Navio

Automatically open Google Maps links in Apple Maps

Get Started

1. Open Settings on your device
2. Go to Safari → Extensions
3. Enable Navio
4. Allow Navio to run on All Websites for full functionality

Note: Navio needs "All Websites" permission to work on all Google domains (google.com, google.co.uk, etc.). The extension only runs on Google pages and does not collect or store any data.

  • One-tap navigation from Google Search to Apple Maps
  • Works on all Google country domains
  • Supports addresses, places, and directions
  • Smart fallback for edge cases
  • No data collection
